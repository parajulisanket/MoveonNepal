Global Nepali

Celebrating Nepalis around the world who carry our culture, contribute to the economy, and make us proud globally.

Remittance Power

Billions of dollars are sent home each year by migrant workers, significantly supporting their families’ daily needs, uplifting communities, and playing a vital role in strengthening the national economy.

Cultural Ambassadors

Nepalis living abroad actively preserve and promote their rich language, vibrant festivals, and unique cultural traditions, sharing them globally and fostering cross-cultural understanding and pride in their heritage.

Global Entrepreneurs

From technology startups to international trade ventures, Nepalis around the world are launching innovative businesses, generating employment opportunities, driving economic growth, and showcasing their talent on a global stage.

Questions About Life Abroad

+

How many Nepali are in abroad?

More than 20 years later, nearly 2.1 million Nepalis live outside Nepal according to the 2021 census, comprising 7.4 percent of the total population. Almost a fifth of them are women, and the median age of all workers was just 28. In 2023, Nepali workers sent home US$ 11 billion in remittances
+

Which country is best for Nepali?

Australia has become a top choice for Nepali students seeking quality education and work opportunities. With its world-class universities, multicultural environment, and student-friendly policies, Australia offers a unique blend of academic excellence and practical experience
+

Which country is best for study?

Several countries are popular for studying abroad, each offering unique advantages. For a broader global perspective and high-quality education, the United States, UK, Canada, and Australia are frequently cited. Canada stands out for its welcoming atmosphere, strong post-study work permit policies, and affordable tuition fees. Germany is also a top contender, especially for engineering and technology, with excellent career opportunities.
