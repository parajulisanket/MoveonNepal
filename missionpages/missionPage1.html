RISHI DHAMALA

JOURNALIST

Rishi Dhamala is a prominent Nepali journalist known for his bold interviewing style and deep involvement in Nepalese political journalism...

Rishi Dhamala is a prominent Nepali journalist known for his bold interviewing style and deep involvement in Nepalese political journalism. He has been a significant figure in the media landscape, particularly through his work with Dhamala Ko Hamala, a popular YouTube channel that features interviews with various political leaders and public figures. His approach often includes direct questioning and a focus on accountability, making him a notable personality in the field of journalism in Nepal.

Journalism Career

Dhamala's career spans over two decades, during which he has become a household name in Nepal. He is the founder and chairperson of the Reporters Club Nepal, a platform that has become central to political discourse in the country. He hosts several talk shows, including "Dhamala Ko Hamala" and "Janata Janna Chahanchhan," known for their direct and sometimes confrontational interviewing style.His commitment to journalism is evident in his rigorous work schedule, often extending to 18-hour days. Dhamala's approach has attracted both admiration and criticism, with some praising his dedication and others questioning his methods.

Media Presence & Public Image

Beyond journalism, Dhamala has ventured into acting, appearing in films such as "Viral Gorkhey" (2025) and "How Funny" (2016). His public persona is characterized by a dramatic and assertive demeanor, which he acknowledges as part of his on-screen character. This has made him a subject of both satire and serious discussion in Nepali media circles.
