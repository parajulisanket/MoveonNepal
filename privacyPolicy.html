Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2025

1. Introduction

At moveOn Nepal, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Such as your name and email when you subscribe or contact us.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, browser type, and IP address.
  • Cookies: For analytics and ad personalization.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect to:

  • Send you breaking news alerts and newsletters
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Personalize ads and content

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with third-party service providers for analytics, email delivery, or legal reasons.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to track user behavior and enhance your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at:

privacy@moveonnepal.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with the new effective date.
