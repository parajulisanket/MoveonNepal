BUILDING THE ECONOMY

Small Businesses: The Engines of the Economy

President Biden is driving a small business boom with American entrepreneurs filing a record 21 million new business applications since he took office.

Historically Strong and Broadly Shared Recovery

The Biden-Harris Administration, thanks in part through the American Rescue Plan, got America vaccinated and out of the pandemic while fueling a historic recovery with a record 16.6 million jobs created, a record 21 million new business applications, and the lowest average unemployment of any administration in more than 50 years.

Most Pro-Labor President in History

Thanks to President Biden's work to fight for overtime pay, protect millions of pensions, and stand in solidarity with workers as they went on strike, the Biden-Harris Administration has been cemented as the most pro-labor administration in history.

Lowering Costs, Tackling Junk Fees, and Promoting Competition

President Biden knows that prices are too high and too many families are being squeezed by the cost of living. The Biden-Harris Administration's actions have lowered costs in key areas – from health insurance premiums and prescription drugs to utility bills.

Building a Fairer Tax System

The Biden-Harris Administration enacted a corporate minimum tax that ensures billion-dollar companies can't get away with paying $0 in federal income taxes, places a surcharge on corporate stock buybacks, and invests in the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) to ensure wealthy tax cheats pay their fair share.

Increased Housing for Americans

After decades of under-investment in housing, we are finally seeing progress under President Biden and Vice President Harris with more units under construction than at any time in over 50 years, and the rate of new housing starts up 17% compared to under the last administration.
